# English-to-Chinese Business Content Adaptation — Worked Example

> Session: 2026-05-08  
> Source: English article about AI automation consulting business  
> Output: 公众号文章《现在市场上有一个缺口，而且这个缺口正在每一周不断扩大》

## Source Material

An English article (~4,000 words) titled with the core thesis that there's a growing market gap between businesses needing AI automation and builders who can deliver it. The article laid out a 90-day roadmap across 6 phases.

## Adaptation Decisions

### What we kept
- The 6-phase structure (the skeleton is universal)
- Core pricing logic (value-based, not hourly)
- The urgency/opportunity framing (this resonates in any market)
- The tech stack simplicity argument (only 5 tools needed)
- The 3 pitfalls (perfectionism, underpricing, no niche)

### What we changed

| Change | Why |
|--------|-----|
| $3K-$15K → ¥5K-¥50K | Localized pricing with PPP adjustment |
| Claude Code/MCP → Claude/Cursor/飞书 | Chinese tools dominate the market |
| Cal.com → 腾讯会议 | Cal.com not widely used in China |
| Loom → 微信视频 | Loom blocked in China |
| Stripe → 微信支付/支付宝 | Payment method of choice |
| LinkedIn → 脉脉/小红书 | LinkedIn China exists but different |
| "Cold email" → "私信" | Social messaging is the primary channel |
| English direct tone → Chinese blog tone | Cultural communication style difference |

### Localization details

**Pricing chain:**
```
原文: $3,000-$15,000 per project
     × 6 汇率 = ¥18,000-¥90,000
     × 0.3-0.5 PPP factor = ¥5,400-¥45,000
     取整 → ¥5,000-¥50,000
     维护费: $500-$1,000 → ¥3,000-¥5,000
     月收入: $13,750 → ¥45,000 (round to 5万)
     年收入: $165,000 → ¥540,000 (round to 54万)
```

**Tool replacement table:**
| English | Chinese | Reasoning |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Claude Code | Claude / Cursor / 任何 AI 编程工具 | Keep brand names that work in China |
| Notion | Notion / Obsidian / 飞书 | Give options, 飞书 is more common |
| Cal.com | 腾讯会议 / 飞书 | Both free and widely used |
| Loom | 微信视频 | Universal in China |
| Stripe | 微信支付 / 支付宝 | The two dominant Chinese payment systems |

**Marketing channel adaptation:**
```
英文渠道：
LinkedIn → 专业内容
Twitter → 行业动态
Cold Email → 直接触达

中文渠道：
小红书 → 实操内容 + 引流
朋友圈 → 信任转化
私信 → 直接触达（代替 Cold Email）
```

## Output Style

- **Language**: Conversational Mandarin with short paragraphs
- **Formatting**: Bold for key numbers, blank lines between sections
- **Headlines**: 二级标题 for phase demarcation, 三级标题 for sub-sections
- **Tone**: Direct but not aggressive; matches Chinese business blog conventions
- **Length**: ~2,500 Chinese characters (appropriate for 公众号)

## Template for Future Adaptations

When adapting English business content for Chinese platforms:

```
1. READ through completely (understand the skeleton)
2. IDENTIFY the universal vs. culture-specific elements
3. MAP the pricing (see formula above)
4. REPLACE tools with Chinese equivalents
5. ADAPT channels (LinkedIn → 小红书, etc.)
6. REWRITE in Chinese blog tone (shorter paragraphs, bold key data)
7. VERIFY cultural references make sense
```
