# Technical Career Coaching: Industrial Engineer Context

Captured from session with Jerry (K&S Suzhou, 2026-06-21).

## Engineer Profile
- **Role**: C++/Python engineer at K&S (Ball Bonder equipment software)
- **Tech stack**: C++ backend, Qt4/5 frontend, some non-Qt (MFC/Win32 legacy)
- **Tenure**: ~1 year, initially reading code line-by-line
- **Current state**: Uses AI for debugging/refactoring, feels skills are "degrading" but understands system architecture better than ever
- **Side project**: Building a personal RAG knowledge base (rag-agent) with semiconductor equipment content

## The Core Insight

Jerry said: **"AI 让我们这些初级工程师更快的向产品经理方向转变了。"**

This is the reframe. The engineer is not losing skills — they are changing phases. The skills that matter shift from:
- Phase 1 (traditional): reading code, writing code, debugging line by line
- Phase 2 (AI-augmented): defining system architecture, connecting modules, evaluating AI outputs
- Phase 3 (product): understanding customer needs, designing solutions within framework constraints, anticipating pain points

## The Propulsion Pattern

Jerry's exact situation maps to a repeatable pattern:

### Starting Conditions
- Works at a traditional industrial company (semiconductor equipment)
- Tech stack is old (Qt4), company is risk-averse, stability over innovation
- Has personal AI side projects (rag-agent knowledge base)
- Has access to valuable domain data (customer logs, alarm codes, equipment manuals)
- Sees the gap: "高层可能会感兴趣" but "现在不是我这个岗位要做的"

### The Four Steps (as they played out in conversation)

| Step | What Jerry did | Implicit skill |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| 1. Build privately | Self-hosted rag-agent, 447 articles + 2500 images, C++/SECS-GEM content | Technical execution, no budget needed |
| 2. Validate silently | Uses it for personal knowledge retrieval | Self-validation, no need for buy-in |
| 3. Seed the idea | "如果开会聊到AI使用的话题，我会提一下" | Strategic patience, organizational awareness |
| 4. Let the role find you | "说不定会让我转岗，专门做AI在公司的落地" | Career vision, defining own role |

### Why This Works for Industrial Companies
Traditional industrial companies (K&S, ASM, Siemens, ABB) share these traits:
- Risk-averse, stable-first culture
- No "AI Engineer" job titles on the org chart
- Middle managers don't know what AI can do — can't request it
- **But**: they have domain data that nobody is using
- **So**: the engineer who demonstrates AI on their own data without asking permission = first mover advantage

### Key Nuances
- Timing matters: Jerry said "现在没办法做" — he knows the company politics. The coach must NOT push.
- The coach's job is to help the engineer SEE the pattern, not push them to act on it.
- Share trigger: "如果我们要展示的话" vs "你应该主动展示" — the former is organic, the latter feels forced.
