# 已验证的通用标题公式目录

> 来源: Buffer.com/resources/headline-formulas (Kevan Lee, 2014) + 《Made to Stick》(Chip & Dan Heath, Random House, 2007) + 《Influence》(Robert Cialdini, 1984) + 行业共识。详见 skill root 的 `title-formulas-research.md`（会话产物）。

## 本质原理

- **David Ogilvy准则**: 平均5倍的人读标题胜过读正文
- **KISSmetrics研究**: 读者吸收标题的「前3词+后3词」，6词标题最佳
- **Copyblogger**: 80%的人读标题，只有20%的人读正文

## 核心公式速查（22个已验证公式）

### 经典框架（宏观）

| 框架 | 来源 | 在标题中的应用 |
|------|------|--------------|
| **SUCCESs** | Chip & Dan Heath, Made to Stick | 简单(Unexpected)+意外+具体+可信+情绪+故事 |
| **Cialdini 7原则** | Robert Cialdini, Influence | 社会认同/稀缺/权威/喜好/互惠 |
| **SHINE** | Bnonn, KISSmetrics | 具体+有用+即时+新闻+娱乐，每篇标题自检 |

### 具体公式

#### 1. 终极标题公式 — Lenka Istvanova, Koozai
```
数字 + 形容词 + 目标关键词 + 理由 + 承诺
```
例: "10 Simple Steps You Can Take Today That Will Make You Happier"

#### 2. [做某事] Like [世界级榜样]
```
[做想做的事] Like [专家] Without [不想有的副作用]
```
例: "Blog Like an All-Star Without Bankrupting Your Free Time" — Joanna Wiebe, Copy Hackers

#### 3. 有趣形容词 + 独特名词 — Jeff Goins
避免"11个方法"式无聊开头。用"令人敬畏的""痛苦的""不容错过的"等强形容词 + 独特名词。

#### 4. [惊艳标题]: 副标题
```
[主标题]: [补充说明]
```
例: "The Fine Art of the Apostrophe: How to Master the Most Difficult Punctuation Problems"

#### 5. SEO标题公式 — Unbounce
```
[形容词] & [形容词] [关键词] That Will [结果承诺]
```
例: "New and Useful Content Marketing Trends That Will Drive You More Traffic"

#### 6. Who Else Wants ___ — John Caples
社会认同 + 群体归属。例: "Who Else Wants an Easier Way to Share to Social Media?"

#### 7. The Secret of ___（___的秘密）
知识稀缺性 + 好奇心。例: "The Secret of Writing Killer Blog Content on a Near-Daily Basis"

#### 8. Little Known Ways to ___（鲜为人知的___方法）
挑战读者知识储备。例: "Little Known Ways to Get More Traffic From Social"

#### 9. Here's a Quick Way to [解决问题] — Brian Clark, Copyblogger
及时 + 实用。例: "Here's a Quick Way to Clean Up Your Profile Page"

#### 10. Have a / Build a ___ You Can Be Proud Of
成就感 + 自我改进。例: "Build an Online Community You Can Be Proud Of"

#### 11. What Everybody Ought to Know About ___
社会证明 + 信息差焦虑。例: "What Everybody Ought to Know About Marketing on Pinterest"

#### 12. [数字] Lessons I Learned From ___ — Sherice Jacobs, Daily Egg
学习路径 + 真实经验。例: "17 Lessons I Learned From Writing a New Blog Post Every Day for a Month"

#### 13. How to Survive Your First ___
路线图 + 安全感。例: "How to Survive Your First 40 Days of Work on a Distributed Team"

#### 14. Peter Sandeen 的 How To 变体
```
How to ___ – The Essential Guide
How to ___ like ___
How to ___ even if / without ___
How to ___ while ___
How to use ___ to ___
How to ___ in five easy steps
```

#### 15. 标题. 额外亮点 — Pat Flynn / Jerod Morris (Copyblogger)
```
主标题. 额外具体亮点
```
例: "13 Awesome Ways to Build a Following on Facebook. We're Trying #5 Today."

#### 16. 迷你标题（4词以内）— Leo Babauta, Zen Habits
极简 = 自信 + 神秘。例: "AI日记. 第7天."

#### 17. Double Whammy（双重标题）— Buffer内部
```
[标题1] + [标题2]
```
例: "14 Advanced Twitter Tips: 14 Strategies to Get the Most Out of Twitter"

#### 18. Amuse-Bouche（开胃菜标题）— Buffer内部
```
[具体条目]和[具体条目]：[列表标题]
```
例: "The Burrito Principle and Beyond: 12 New Marketing Ideas That Are Memorable, Actionable, and Useful"

#### 19. ____, Backed By Science — Buffer内部
背书感 + 信任。例: "Happiness Hacks: The 10 Most Unexpected Ways to Be Happy, Backed By Science"

#### 20. The Ultimate Guide / The Beginner's Guide to ___
完整感 + 低门槛。例: "打工人AI入门完全指南"

## 小红书/自媒体特定方向（行业共识）

| 方向 | 模板 | 心理触发 |
|------|------|---------|
| **日记体** | 打工人第X天｜主题 | 代入感+陪伴感 |
| **反差式** | 下班前XXX，下班后XXX | 身份冲突+好奇心 |
| **实验叙事** | 我试了XXX天｜实测 | 时间投入=可信 |
| **颠覆认知** | 不是XXX是XXX | 认知失调→点击验证 |
| **打工人共鸣** | 工资没涨但XXX | 共情+自嘲 |
| **情绪留白** | 具体场景 + 开放式结尾 | 悬念+追更欲 |
